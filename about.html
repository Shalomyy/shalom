ABOUT

Shalom Yemane, a digital artist, delves into the realm of dark femininity and sadness to craft a narrative where shadow and light intertwine in a dance of profound introspection. Within the cocoon of melancholy, I discover solace and draw inspiration, utilizing my artistic expressions as a conduit to encapsulate the emotional depths that surface during moments of solitude and vulnerability.


My artworks serve as an invitation, beckoning viewers to immerse themselves in a world where the dichotomy of darkness and light converges harmoniously. In this space, vulnerability is not only acknowledged but celebrated, and sadness finds solace within the transformative power of art. Each piece becomes a journey, a testament to the strength that can be discovered within the depths of the gloom.
